WHO WE ARE

Creating Nepal's Next Generation of Democratic Leaders

Loktantra Lab is a youth-led civic education initiative founded in Lalitpur, Nepal. Through immersive workshops, policy simulations, civic innovation labs, and community partnerships, we equip young people with the knowledge and confidence to participate meaningfully in democratic life.

Democracy

We believe democracy is not only learned from textbooks, but through experience, dialogue, participation, and responsible action.

Civic Innovation

Young people solve real community problems through design thinking, policy innovation, and collaborative leadership.

Community

We partner with schools, youth organisations, and local communities to build sustainable civic engagement.

Accountability

Our workshops teach participants practical ways to engage elected representatives, monitor policies, and participate peacefully.

WHAT WE DO

Our Programs

Experiential civic education that transforms democratic ideas into practical skills.

Mock Elections

Participants experience the complete democratic process—from voter registration and campaigning to voting, ballot counting, and election analysis.

Mock Parliament

Students debate real national and local issues while learning parliamentary procedure, coalition-building, respectful dialogue, and policymaking.

Policy Innovation Labs

Young people work together to develop creative solutions for issues affecting their own communities.

Advocacy Workshops

Learn how to contact elected representatives, write advocacy letters, submit petitions, and participate responsibly in civic life.

Youth Civic Action Groups

Building long-term community leadership through youth-led civic groups and collaborative local initiatives.

Civic Education Resources

Developing bilingual educational materials that can continue to be used by schools and community organisations across Nepal.

FOUNDER

David Khanal

Founder • Loktantra Lab

David Khanal is a student at UWC Maastricht and the founder of Loktantra Lab. Inspired by Nepal's democratic journey and passionate about civic innovation, David created Loktantra Lab to empower young people with practical democratic skills that extend beyond the classroom.

His work has been recognised through the Go Make A Difference Award and has been featured by UWC Maastricht for his commitment to civic leadership and community impact.
