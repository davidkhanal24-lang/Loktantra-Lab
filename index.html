About

Loktantra Lab is a youth-focused civic education initiative that helps participants better understand democracy, governance, leadership, and active citizenship through engaging workshops, discussions, and collaborative learning.

Our Goals

Civic Literacy

Increase understanding of democratic institutions.

Youth Leadership

Build confidence and leadership skills.

Community Action

Encourage informed participation in local communities.

Activities

Interactive Workshops

Mock Parliament Sessions

Policy Discussions

Community Projects

Why It Matters

Strong democracies depend on informed citizens. By helping young people understand governance and participate constructively, Loktantra Lab aims to strengthen civic engagement and inspire positive change.
